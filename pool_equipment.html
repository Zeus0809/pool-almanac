Pool Equipment

Let’s familiarize ourselves with different kinds of equipment used when playing pool. To be able to play a game, you will need a pool table, a set of pool balls, a triangle, a pool cue, and chalk. This is the minimum supply needed by one player to shoot. However, depending on the kind of game, level of players, and other factors, equipment may vary.
